The arithmetic operator block can be found under the "numbers" menu. It consists of two blank slots for numbers or variables and a dropdown to select the operator.
Fill in the slots with variables, numbers, or more arithmetic operation blocks.
Choose the arithmetic operation you want to use.
The operation will be simplified for printing or storing to a variable.
