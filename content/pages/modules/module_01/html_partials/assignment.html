Module 01 Assignment

Create a game with Scratch or a Turtle drawing with Trinket.io

Your game or turle drawing must include:
  • Input from the user
  • Variables and assignment operators
  • At least one of each: arithmetic operators and comparison operators
  • If statements

Keep in mind that we are doing standards-based grading, so demonstrate the extent of your knowledge by applying the concepts creatively.
Your assignment document should include
  1. Your First and Last Name
  2. A link to your project on Scratch or Trinket
  3. A reflection on your process creating this project. Think about what went well, what pitfalls you encountered, etc.
