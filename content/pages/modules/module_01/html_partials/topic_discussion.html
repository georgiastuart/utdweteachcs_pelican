Discussion Part 2

Part 2 Ends on Sunday, March 26th at 11:59 pm

There was a lot of great discussion this week! I noticed three major concerns:
  • Difficulty implementing extra stuff into an already packed curriculum
  • Concerns of the feasibility across student abilities (either to difficult or too simplistic)
  • Technology limitations
With that in mind, part 2 of the discussion is a more general discussion on implementing new tools in your classroom. Please answer at least one of the following:
  1. How much "teaching the tool" time do you find acceptable for integrating a new technology or instructional technique into your classroom? Does this change depending on time of year?
  2. What are some strategies you use to teach to a broad spectrum of abilities (not just for tech, but for your subject as well)? Do you see a way this could be applied to new tech tools?
